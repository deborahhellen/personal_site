About


Deborah is a life-long horsewoman and passionate dressage rider currently located in Needham, MA. She grew up riding with Tami Batts in North Carolina and relocated to Massachusetts in 2012 to pursue an engineering degree at Olin College. She received her USDF Bronze Medal in the summer of 2012 and completed the USDF "L" Program in 2015. She takes pride in introducing horses of all breeds to dressage and developing a happy athlete through kind, correct training.

Deborah is available for judging dressage schooling shows in the New England area during the fall and spring. Dressage training is also available on a limited basis. Please inquire below for further information about availability and pricing!

She also designs and builds custom websites for individuals or small businesses. Please inquire for further information on pricing and website options!
